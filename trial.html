Climate Questions in North America

1. What is the average annual temperature in North America?

Average annual temperatures vary across the continent, ranging from below freezing in the northern regions to over 80°F (27°C) in southern areas.

2. How does climate change impact weather patterns in North America?

Climate change is causing more extreme weather events, including increased hurricanes, droughts, and heavy rainfall.

3. What are the main climate zones in North America?

North America features various climate zones including arctic, temperate, and tropical zones.

4. How do ocean currents affect the climate of North America?

Ocean currents, such as the Gulf Stream, play a crucial role in moderating temperatures along the eastern coast of North America.

5. What is the effect of deforestation on climate in North America?

Deforestation contributes to climate change by increasing greenhouse gas emissions and disrupting local climate patterns.
